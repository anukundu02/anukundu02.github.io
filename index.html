About Me

I am working as an Assistant Research Scientist for UMBC at NASA/GSFC, under the CRESST II cooperative agreement.

I specialize in high-energy astrophysics, majorly focusing on neutron stars/pulsars, their emission mechanisms and magnetic field configuration modelling, PWN (pulsar wind nebula) modelling. I also have experience with processing radio data from GMRT and MeerKAT telescopes.

I am a passionate programmer, and have worked with the following languages: C++, C, Python, Fortran, Mathematica, MATLAB. I am experienced with and interested in parallel programming, high performance computation, and advanced computational techniques.
